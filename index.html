Brew Bites

Search for a dish or an ingredient and beers that have a matching dish will be displayed. Each beer is displayed with: Name, Tagline (think catchy one liner description), ABV (alcohol content in %) and IBU's (bitterness scale; lower is less bitter - light beers are around 45-50). Clicking on a beer will display a full description and a dish to go with it based on your search. Recipes include clickable images (takes you to the recipe site) ingredients and time to make.
